About Me

 
    •  Hi I'm Ben. I'm a Masters of Engineering student in Mechanical Engineering at Cornell University. I also did my undergrad at Cornell in Mechanical Engineering and minored in Computer Science. I have the pleasure of working in the EmPRISE Lab with Professor Tapomayukh Bhattacharjee where I work on robot assistive feeding. I was previously the arm lead of the Cornell Mars Rover team and competed in the University Rover Challenge. I'm passionate about robots. My focus is on the fusion between hardware and software for the development of the next generation of robotics for learning and manipulation.
